Elvish
-Tonal
-Limited consonants
-Lots of vowels
-Alphabet worked around the tones.
-One tone per word "piece"

Consonants - K, Kh, M, N, Zh, J, H, S(h), L, Q, W, B, Ng, Y, D, Dh
Vowels Tones -
Neutral - A, E, I, O, U
Middle - Á, É, Í, Ó, Ú
Hard - Ä, Ö, Ü 
Other - A'a, Ou, Eu, E'e, Ii

Wood Elves (lit. Spirit Elf) - Khämu'Án
High Elves (lit. Elf from Silver) - JīnZhá'Án - If second vowel tone doesn't match, its dropped (to avoid two 'á, á').
